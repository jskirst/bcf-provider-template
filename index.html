Welcome to East Academy Preschool & Day Care

A Christ centered Montessori school dedicated to the love and joy of learning.

About Us

Kidus Montessori is committed to the development of the child: physical, mental, emotional, social, spiritual, and aesthetic. In order to help the "whole child" grow and develop, we focus on a Christ centered world view, delivered through the Montessori method of education.

Our Mission

At Kidus Montessori, our primary mission is to nurture your child’s growth. An equally important mission is to foster and build a vibrant, supportive and involved community of parents, teachers and families.

Our Goal

For children to have a love and joy for learning. Our Christ-centered environment is a way of life through love, compassion and respect. We strive to always consider the long-term welfare of your child’s physical, mental, emotional, social, spiritual, and aesthetic development focusing on following the spirit of the child.

Enrollment

How to join our Kidus Family

Please call to schedule a two hour tour of our learning environment. We look forward to you observing our classrooms and meeting with our Director/Administrator. A three day transition period is required.

Admission Forms

There are 13 forms you are required to complete prior to your child’s attendance.

  1. Child Care Home Register
  2. Certificate of Immunization Status (to be updated yearly)
  3. Permission Authorization (for field trips, etc.)
  4. Child Care Agreement or Child Care Services Agreement
  5. Consent for Medical Care and Treatment
  6. Medication/Treatment Authorization (when applicable)
  7. Annual Registration fee
  8. Statement of Faith Focus
  9. Discipline Policy
Trial Period

The trial period will be up to 6 ks. This period is used to observe the child’s adjustment to care and to talk about concerns; feel free to call. Please tell me if you have any concerns. After a 4 week trial period, we will determine if day care services are satisfactory to everyone. If any problems cannot be resolved then care is terminated.
