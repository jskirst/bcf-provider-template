Enrollment

Please call or fill out the contact form below to schedule a tour of our loving school. During the tour, you will have a chance to observe teachers and tour the classrooms. Every tour ends with a personal meeting with our Director to discuss our philosophy and the individual needs of your child.

Admission Forms

In order to enroll, you'll need to provide each of the following:

  1. Certificate of Immunization Status
  2. Permission Authorization
  3. Child Care Agreement or Child Care Services Agreement
  4. Consent for Medical Care and Treatment
  5. Statement of Faith Focus
Trial Period

We know that each child is unique, and our school and philosophy is not a perfect fit for everyone. With that in mind, we ask families to engage in a 3 week trial period where your child will have the chance to take advantage of all we have to offer, while our team of expert teachers observes their adjustment and growth in our care. At the end of the 3 weeks, if our teachers and your family agree that our school is a good match, they will be fully admitted into our care.
